Screen Ledger — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

Screen Ledger ("the app"), published by Sycamore Hill Studios, is a local-only app.

  • No data collection. The app does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All app data stays on your device.
  • No accounts, no analytics, no ads. The app requires no sign-up and contains no advertising or analytics SDKs.
  • Purchases. Optional tips are processed entirely by Google Play's billing system. The app never sees or stores your payment details. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Deleting data. Uninstalling the app removes all of its data.
